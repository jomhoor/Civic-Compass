# Civic Compass - Questionnaire Research & Licensing

## 1. Executive Summary & Recommendation
For a **Freemium/Commercial App** (Citizen Sponsor Tier), strict copyright compliance is required.

*   **✅ SAFE:** **IPIP (International Personality Item Pool)**. It is explicitly Public Domain.
*   **⚠️ CAUTION:** **PolitiScales / SapplyValues**. Open source code (MIT) usually implies open content, but verify specific text licenses.
*   **❌ AVOID:** **European Social Survey (ESS)**. It is **CC BY-NC-SA (Non-Commercial)**. Using this would legally block you from charging for the app or premium features without a separate license.

**Best Strategy:** Use IPIP for personality traits. For the political compass, assume the *methodology* (8 axes) is free, but **rewrite the specific propositions** to create your own unique "Civic Compass" IP. This avoids copyright issues and allows you to tailor the language to your specific "Civic" vs "Political" tone.

## 3. License Notes & Action Items

### A. IPIP (Personality & Motivation)
*   **Status:** Gold Standard.
*   **Action:** You can copy these items verbatim.
*   **Source:** [ipip.ori.org](https://ipip.ori.org)

### B. Political Compass / PolitiScales
*   **Status:** The "Political Compass" trademark is owned by Pace News Ltd. Do not use their exact questions or name.
*   **Action:** Use **PolitiScales** (MIT Licensed) or **SapplyValues** (MIT Licensed) as the *structural basis*.
*   **Recommendation:** Rewrite the questions.
    *   *Original:* "Military action that defies international law is sometimes justified."
    *   *Your Version:* "Interventionism is necessary when international bodies fail to act." (Same vector, new IP).

### C. Attribution
Even for Public Domain/MIT, always include an "Acknowledgements" screen in the app:
> *"Psychometric methodology adapted from the International Personality Item Pool (IPIP) and PolitiScales open-source project."*
